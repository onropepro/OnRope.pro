# White Label Branding

Customize the platform with your company logo and brand colors.

## Overview

The White Label Branding feature allows rope access companies to customize OnRopePro with their own branding. Display your company logo, apply your brand colors, and present a professional, cohesive appearance to employees and clients. This subscription-gated feature enhances your company's professional image.

## Key Features

### Custom Logo
- Upload your company logo
- Logo appears in the application header
- Logo included on PDF exports (quotes, reports)
- Replaces default OnRopePro branding

### Brand Colors
- Set primary brand color
- Colors applied throughout the interface
- Consistent brand experience
- Maintains visual hierarchy

### PDF Branding
When exporting documents:
- Quotes display your logo
- Reports show company branding
- Professional presentation to clients
- Consistent brand identity

## How It Works

### Setting Up Branding
1. Navigate to Company Settings
2. Select Branding tab
3. Upload company logo
4. Choose brand colors
5. Preview changes
6. Save settings

### Logo Requirements
- Recommended: PNG or SVG format
- Transparent background preferred
- Reasonable file size for performance
- Appropriate aspect ratio for header display

### Color Selection
- Enter hex color code or use color picker
- Preview shows how color appears
- Colors applied to buttons, accents, highlights
- Two-color limit ensures consistent, professional appearance

## Subscription Requirement

White Label Branding is a $49/month add-on available to all paying subscribers. When your subscription expires or is cancelled, branding automatically reverts to default OnRopePro styling (your configuration is preserved for easy reactivation).

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I preview before saving?**
A: Yes, preview mode shows how branding will appear.

**Q: Does branding appear on mobile?**
A: Yes, branding is responsive across all devices.

**Q: What if I change my branding later?**
A: Update anytime; new branding applies immediately.

**Q: Do previously exported PDFs update?**
A: No, exported PDFs retain the branding at time of export.
