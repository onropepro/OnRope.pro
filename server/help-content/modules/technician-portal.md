# Technician Portal

Your personal dashboard for managing your rope access career, certifications, and employer connections.

## Overview

The Technician Portal is your home base in OnRopePro. Whether you're an independent technician looking for work or already connected to an employer, this is where you manage your profile, track certifications, view job opportunities, and maintain your safety records.

## Getting Started

### Profile Completion

Complete your profile to unlock full access to the Job Board and make yourself visible to employers:

1. **Personal Information**: Add your name, contact details, and location
2. **Certifications**: Upload your IRATA or SPRAT certifications
3. **Work Preferences**: Set your availability and preferred job types
4. **Resume/CV**: Upload your resume for job applications
5. **Profile Photo**: Add a professional photo for employer visibility

**Tip**: A complete profile is 5x more likely to receive job offers from employers.

### IRATA/SPRAT Certification Tracking

Track your certification levels and expiration dates:
- Level 1, 2, or 3 certifications
- Expiration date reminders
- Revalidation scheduling
- Continuing education tracking

## Employer Connections

### Linking with an Employer

When you join a rope access company using OnRopePro:
1. Your employer sends you a connection request
2. Accept the invitation in your portal
3. You gain access to company-specific features:
   - Project assignments
   - Work hour tracking
   - Safety form submissions
   - Equipment checkout
   - Payroll information

### Unlinked Technician Features

Even without an employer connection, you can:
- Complete and maintain your profile
- Track your own certifications
- Browse the Job Board
- Apply to job postings
- Receive direct offers from employers
- Access training resources

## Job Board Access

### Finding Work

The Job Board connects you with verified rope access employers:
- Browse active job postings
- Filter by location, job type, and pay range
- One-click applications with auto-filled profile data
- Track application status

### Profile Visibility

Control who sees your profile:
- Toggle visibility to employers
- Set your expected pay rate
- Choose which certifications to display
- Update availability status

### Direct Offers

Employers can send you job offers directly:
- Receive notifications in your portal
- Review offer details
- Accept or decline with one tap
- Track all received offers

## Referral Program

2. When someone registers using your code, you both benefit
3. Track your referrals and rewards
4. Premium referrers unlock PLUS features

**Your Referral Code**: Available in your profile and header bar

## Common Questions

**Q: How do I become visible to employers?**
A: Complete your profile and enable the "Visible to Employers" toggle in your profile settings.

**Q: Can I work for multiple employers?**
A: Yes, you can accept connections from multiple employers simultaneously.

**Q: How do I track my work hours?**
A: Once connected to an employer, use the clock-in feature on assigned projects.

**Q: What certifications should I upload?**
A: Upload all current IRATA or SPRAT certifications, plus any relevant safety training certificates.
