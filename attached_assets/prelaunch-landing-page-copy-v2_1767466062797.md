## 19 Modules Section

**Headline:** 19 Modules. One Platform. All Connected.

**Subhead:** Everything you need to run a profitable rope access company, from first quote to final payment and every drop in between. Complex operations made simple through absolute transparency.

### Module Categories

**Operations (5):**
1. Project Management - 4-elevation tracking with visual progress bars and crew assignment
2. Work Sessions - GPS clock-in, drop tracking, automatic hour aggregation
3. Scheduling - Drag-and-drop calendar with conflict detection and weather delays
4. Gear Inventory - Serial number tracking, assignment history, retirement workflows
5. White Label - Custom branding for client-facing portals and exports

**Safety (5):**
1. Safety Compliance - Digital forms, PDF plans, and audit-ready documentation
2. Company Safety Rating - Real-time company safety score based on inspections and compliance
3. Personal Safety Rating - Portable technician safety score that follows you across employers
4. IRATA/SPRAT Logging - Same-day hour logging with OCR import and career-portable records
5. Documents - Digital signatures, immutable audit trails, compliance reporting

**Team (4):**
1. Employee Management - Portable identities, certification tracking, role-based permissions
2. Technician Passport - Portable work history and certifications across employers
3. Job Board - Talent browser with unlimited postings and direct offers
4. User Access - Granular permissions and audit trails for every action

**Financial (3):**
1. Payroll - Automatic hour aggregation with overtime calculation and exports
2. Quoting - Multi-service quotes with photo documentation and historical data
3. CRM - Client relationship management with contact history and notes

**Communication (2):**
1. Resident Portal - Progress tracking and feedback submission for building residents
2. Property Manager Portal - Real-time dashboards and compliance exports for property managers

### Connectivity Callout

> **Every module talks to every other module.** A work session updates payroll, progress tracking, performance analytics, and safety compliance automatically. Create a client once, they're in your system forever. Create a project once, it pulls from your history. Information flows where it needs to go, when it needs to get there.
