# Tech Plus Account: Value Comparison

**Document Type:** Feature Comparison  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** December 8, 2025  
**Purpose:** Show value of Plus account with and without employer on platform

---

## Feature Comparison Table

| Feature | WITHOUT Employer on Platform | WITH Employer on Platform |
|---------|------------------------------|---------------------------|
| **Job Board Access** | ✅ Full access. Browse & apply to REAL rope access jobs from REAL rope access companies. | ✅ Same + employers can see your verified profile and reach out directly. |
| **Document Vault** | ✅ Store certs, banking info, void cheque, SIN. Encrypted, organized. | ✅ One-click share to employer on connection. Zero re-entry. |
| **Logbook** | ✅ Take photos of your physical logbook pages. They convert to text and are saved as part of your history. | ✅ Logbook scanning + automatic detailed logging of activities to your digital book. No more trying to remember what you did when. |
| **Exportable Work History PDF** | ✅ From scanned logbook data. Useful for job apps. | ✅ Rich data: "178 rope transfers, 3,500 deviations, 2,000 drops" with dates, buildings, tasks. |
| **30-Day Cert Expiry Alerts** | ✅ You get notified before IRATA/SPRAT lapses. | ✅ You AND employer get notified. No surprises. |
| **Resume Storage** | ✅ Upload and store. Ready when you need it. | ✅ Same. |
| **PLUS Badge** | ✅ Shows on your profile. | ✅ Employers see you're serious about your career. |
| **Unlimited Employer Connections** | ⚠️ Feature exists, but no employers to connect to yet. | ✅ Work multiple companies. One profile, all jobs. |
| **Safety Rating (0-100)** | ❌ Not available. Requires daily harness inspections + signed docs. | ✅ Valuable CSR score builds from simplified compliance behaviour. Visible to future employers. |
| **Work History Analytics** | ⚠️ Limited to scanned logbook data. | ✅ Detailed lifetime totals by task type, building, employer. All tracked and logged automatically. |
| **Automatic Hour Logging** | ❌ Not available. | ✅ Clock in/out. Hours submit themselves. No timesheets. |
| **Instant Onboarding** | ❌ Not available. | ✅ New job? Plug in. Employer enters salary + permissions. Done. Day 1 ready. |
| **Payroll Accuracy** | ❌ Still at mercy of manual systems. | ✅ Verified hours = no disputes. Transparent pay. |

---

## Summary

| Scenario | Value Level | Best For |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **Plus WITHOUT employer** | **Solid standalone value** | Techs who want organized certs, digitized logbook, job board access, and to be ready when employers join. |
| **Plus WITH employer** | **Full platform unlock** | Everything automatic. Hours, certs, logbook, onboarding, safety rating. Handled. |

---

## The Honest Pitch

**Without employer:** "Your professional home base. Certs organized. Logbook digitized. Job board access. Ready for when the industry catches up."

**With employer:** "You work. Everything else is automatic."

---

## Notes

- **CSR** = Compliance & Safety Rating
- Plus is unlocked free with 1 referral (mutual benefit, both techs get upgraded)
- Job board access is a key differentiator for Plus tier
- Logbook scanner works immediately; automatic logging requires employer connection
