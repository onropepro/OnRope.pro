## What Are Leading Metrics?

**Leading metrics** are predictive, controllable indicators that signal future performance. They answer: *"Are we on track to hit our goals?"*

| Characteristic | Leading Metrics | Lagging Metrics |
|----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Timing** | Early signal | After the fact |
| **Control** | Directly controllable | Outcome of actions |
| **Purpose** | Course correction | Performance measurement |
| **Example** | Trial starts | Paid conversions |
