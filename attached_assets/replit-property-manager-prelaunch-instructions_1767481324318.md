# Replit Instructions: Property Manager Pre-Launch Landing Page

**Page URL:** `/property-manager-coming-soon` or `/pm-prelaunch`  
**Reference:** Use existing property manager portal page styling and main pre-launch page structure  
**Purpose:** Pre-launch signup page targeting property & strata managers (not employers or technicians)

---

## Audience Context

Property managers are NOT the paying customers. Employers (rope access companies) pay for the platform. Property managers get free portal access when their vendors use OnRopePro.

**Their motivations:**
- Reduce liability exposure from vendor negligence
- Stop chasing vendor insurance certificates
- Get out of the resident complaint loop
- Have data to justify vendor decisions to building owners
- Look professional and technologically sophisticated

**Their fears:**
- Being blamed when a vendor incident occurs
- Spending hours on admin instead of property management
- Not being able to prove due diligence
- Resident complaints consuming their time

---
