## ðŸ”„ PSR vs CSR Relationship

### Key Distinction

| Metric | PSR (Personal Safety Rating) | CSR (Company Safety Rating) |
|--------|------------------------------|----------------------------|
| **Belongs to** | Individual technician | Company |
| **Portability** | Follows you between employers | Stays with company |
| **Visibility** | Employers see during hiring | Property managers see during vendor selection |
| **Components** | Personal inspections, quizzes, incidents | Company docs, toolbox meetings, team compliance |

### Independence Principle

PSR and CSR are calculated separately. They do not directly impact each other.

**Validated Quote (Lines 48):**
> "We are not going to have it directly impact the CSR. No. But we will have it displayed just for the company owner... the WSS."

### Indirect Relationship

While PSR does not directly feed into CSR, completing personal safety tasks improves both metrics:
- Harness inspections improve your PSR AND contribute to company's inspection compliance (CSR)
- Document signatures improve your PSR AND contribute to company's document review score (CSR)
- Your daily safety behavior benefits both your personal rating and your employer's rating

## ðŸ“ˆ Workforce Safety Score (WSS)

### What It Is

The Workforce Safety Score displays the average PSR of all employees linked to a company.

**Formula:**
```
WSS = Sum of All Employee PSRs / Number of Employees
```

### Who Sees It

### Purpose

WSS is an **informational metric only**. It helps owners:
- Evaluate candidates during hiring (compare PSR to team average)
- Identify employees who need safety coaching
- Track team safety culture over time
- Make data-driven decisions about training investments

### Important: WSS Does NOT Impact CSR

## ðŸ“– Terminology & Naming

### Core Terms

**PSR (Personal Safety Rating)**
- **Definition:** Individual technician's safety compliance score calculated from certifications, inspections, quizzes, and work history
- **Portability:** Follows technician between employers
- **Range:** 0-100%

**WSS (Workforce Safety Score)**
- **Definition:** Average PSR of all employees linked to a company
- **Visibility:** Company owner and operations managers only
- **Purpose:** Informational metric for hiring and coaching decisions

**CSR (Company Safety Rating)**
- **Definition:** Company-level compliance score based on documentation, toolbox meetings, and team inspection compliance
- **Relationship to PSR:** Calculated separately, no direct impact
- **Visibility:** Property managers can view for vendor selection

### What We DON'T Call It (And Why)

**"Safety Score" vs "Personal Safety Rating"**
- âŒ **NOT:** "Safety Score" (too generic, could mean CSR or PSR)
- âœ… **CORRECT:** "PSR" or "Personal Safety Rating"
- **Why:** Clarity between individual (PSR) and company (CSR) metrics

**"Recent Inspections" vs "Lifetime Inspections"**
- âŒ **NOT:** "Based on last 30 days"
- âœ… **CORRECT:** "Based on inspections over time"
- **Why:** PSR tracks career-long behavior, not just recent activity

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### "Why is my quiz count different from my coworker's?"

**Answer:** Quiz availability depends on your certification level and employer connection. A Level 2 tech sees more quizzes than Level 1. When connected to an employer, you also see quizzes for company documents.

**Validated Quote (Lines 9-10):**
> "There's safety quizzes that are always there. The IRATA or SPRAT depending on their score. And then there are the safety quizzes about the company's documents."

---

### "Does my PSR affect my employer's CSR?"

**Answer:** No, PSR and CSR are calculated separately. Your PSR does not directly impact your employer's CSR. However, completing safety tasks (inspections, document signatures) improves both your PSR and contributes to company metrics.

**Validated Quote (Line 48):**
> "We are not going to have it directly impact the CSR."

---

### "What happens to my PSR when I change jobs?"

**Answer:** Your PSR follows you. All inspections, quizzes, and safety history from previous employers remain part of your permanent record. Your new employer sees your complete PSR on day one.

**Validated Quote (Lines 8-9):**
> "If they do a harness inspection while they're working for a company, it counts towards their PSR. Even though if they change company, it stays with them."

---

### "How quickly does my PSR update?"

**Answer:** PSR updates in real-time as you complete safety activities. Complete an inspection and your score recalculates immediately. No waiting period or manual updates required.

---

### "Can I see what's hurting my PSR?"

**Answer:** Yes. Your PSR breaks down into component scores. You can see exactly which component (certifications, safety documents, quizzes, work history) is lower and focus improvement there.

---

### "Why did my score drop after changing employers?"

**Answer:** When you link to a new employer, the Work History component activates (25% weight). If you're new to that employer with no sessions, Work History starts at 50%. This is temporary and improves as you complete sessions without incidents.

---

### "What's the difference between PSR and CSR?"

**Answer:** PSR (Personal Safety Rating) belongs to you as an individual technician. CSR (Company Safety Rating) belongs to the company. PSR tracks your personal safety behavior; CSR tracks company-wide compliance systems.
