## A/B Test Recommendations

**Test 1: Headline Framing**
- A: Loss framing ("Your career doesn't follow you")
- B: Gain framing ("Own your career data forever")
- Why: Loss aversion may outperform, but gain framing could feel more empowering

**Test 2: CTA Copy**
- A: "Create Your Free Profile"
- B: "Claim Your Free Profile"
- C: "Get Started Free"
- Why: "Claim" implies ownership/entitlement; "Get Started" is action-oriented

**Test 3: Social Proof Placement**
- A: Trust signals in hero section
- B: Trust signals after problem section
- Why: May convert better after pain points are established

**Test 4: Referral Framing**
- A: "Share with one tech â†’ you both get Plus"
- B: "Unlock Plus togetherâ€”just one referral"
