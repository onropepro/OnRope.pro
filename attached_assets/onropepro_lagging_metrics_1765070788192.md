## What Are Lagging Metrics?

**Lagging metrics** are outcome indicators that measure results after actions are taken. They answer: *"Did we hit our goals?"*

| Characteristic | Leading Metrics | Lagging Metrics |
|----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Timing** | Early signal | After the fact |
| **Control** | Directly controllable | Outcome of actions |
| **Purpose** | Course correction | Performance measurement |
| **Example** | Trial starts | Revenue |
