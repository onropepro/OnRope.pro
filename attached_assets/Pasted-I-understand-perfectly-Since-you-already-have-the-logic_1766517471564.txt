I understand perfectly. Since you already have the logic and data connections in your other project, the most efficient way to apply this design is to just copy the "App Shell" (the Sidebar and Header structure) and wrap your existing content in it.

Here is the clean, reusable code for the Layout Shell. You can copy this component into your project (e.g., as components/Layout.tsx) and simply pass your existing dashboard content as children.

1. The Layout Component (Layout.tsx)
Copy this file to your project. It contains the Sidebar and Sticky Header.
