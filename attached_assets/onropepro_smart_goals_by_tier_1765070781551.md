## Tier-Specific Acquisition Strategies

### Starter ($299/mo)
- **Channel:** Inbound (SEO, content), referrals
- **Message:** "Stop losing money on payroll errors"
- **Trial:** 90-day free trial, convert on value demonstrated
- **CAC Target:** <$500

### Professional ($549/mo)
- **Channel:** Tech-driven forced adoption, inbound, trade shows
- **Message:** "Run more jobs with less chaos"
- **Trial:** 90-day free trial with onboarding call
- **CAC Target:** <$800

### Enterprise ($999/mo)
- **Channel:** Direct sales, tech-driven adoption, strategic partnerships
- **Message:** "Scale without hiring admin staff"
- **Trial:** 90-day trial with white-glove onboarding
- **CAC Target:** <$1,500

### Unlimited ($1,999/mo)
- **Channel:** Direct sales, upgrade from Enterprise
- **Message:** "Unlimited everything for $50/tech/month"
- **Trial:** 30-day unlimited trial at Enterprise price
- **CAC Target:** <$2,500

## Tier Upgrade Path

```
Starter → Professional: When hitting 8+ techs or 5+ projects/mo
Professional → Enterprise: When hitting 16+ techs or 12+ projects/mo
Enterprise → Unlimited: When hitting 40+ techs or overage costs > $500/mo
```

### Upgrade Trigger Monitoring

| Current Tier | Upgrade Signal | Action |
|--------------|----------------|--------|
| Starter at 7 techs | Approaching seat limit | Proactive outreach |
| Professional at 14 projects | Hitting project cap | Upgrade conversation |
| Enterprise at $400 overage | Unlimited makes sense | ROI comparison |
